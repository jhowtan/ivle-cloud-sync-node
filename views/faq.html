1 What is Cloud Sync?

Cloud Sync is a web service built for IVLE users. Our servers crawl ivle for new, updated files and delivers them directly to your dropbox account in real time.

2 What do I have to do to use Cloud Sync?

All you need to do is sign in once to connect your account to Cloud Sync. The quickstart guide will show you how.

3 Do I have to download any application or programs? Is this available on my desktop computer, laptop, tablet or mobile phone?

You only need to download the Dropbox application on your devices to access all your files.

4 Why Dropbox?

It’s free, easy to use and everyone in NUS has it (see winners of Dropbox Space Race).

Don’t have an account? Click here to sign up.

5 Where will my files be uploaded to?

The default folder would be Dropbox > Apps > IVLE Cloud Sync. Follow the next steps guide on how you can customise your folders.

6 I want to use Cloud Sync in a different way. How do I request for features?

You can send your feedback to ivlecloudsync.at.gmail.com :)

7 Who are the developers behind Cloud Sync?
