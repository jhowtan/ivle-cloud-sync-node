Quick Start

Setup is easy and takes less than a minute to complete

1 Logging in

Cloud Sync requires authorization from your IVLE and Dropbox accounts. We do not store your IVLE or Dropbox passwords, only the application tokens provided to us by IVLE and Dropbox.

2 Selecting folders to sync

By default, Cloud Sync will sync all your folders. We provide the option to exclude certain folders or entire workbins to your liking (but why would anyone do that).

Uncheck the folders you do not want synchronised (remember to submit), and Cloud Sync will handle the rest.

And you're done!

Check out Next Steps: Customising your App Folder to move or rename your IVLE folder on Dropbox.
